Practice Area

i. Corporate Law:

We provide comprehensive services such as business formation and incorporation, drafting project proposals, articles of incorporation, service rules, and nondisclosure agreements. Additionally, we offer expert advice on corporate governance, mergers and acquisitions, and more.

ii. Contract Law:

Handled various civil and electrical contract agreements for both international and national projects at Bhutan Power Corporation Limited. We excel in drafting, reviewing, and negotiating contracts across different sectors, tailoring agreements to meet the specific needs and goals of our clients.

iii. Arbitration

Since 2015, I have served as an arbitrator at the Bhutan Alternative Dispute Resolution Center, successfully arbitrating over 31 cases of varying nature and complexity. Our center specializes in:

  • Drafting and Reviewing: We provide expert services in drafting and reviewing legal documents to ensure clarity and precision.
  • Negotiation and Case Presentation: Our team excels in negotiation strategies and presenting cases effectively before the tribunal.
  • Mediation and Reconciliation: We are trained experts in mediation, negotiation, and reconciliation, offering these as viable alternatives to traditional court proceedings.
iv. Tax Laws:

Taxation is a specialized field demanding unwavering dedication and meticulous attention to detail in comprehending clients' tax matters.
Our expertise extends to advising on business taxes and corporate income taxes for firms and corporations.

v. Family and Inheritance Law:

Family and inheritance cases demand the highest level of compassion and sensitivity. Our firm specializes in handling a wide range of family matters, including divorce, child custody, inheritance, and more, with care and attention, whether through out-of-court negotiations or in-court proceedings.

vi. Penal Laws:

We are committed to safeguarding the rights of our clients, ensuring fair treatment through meticulous adherence to due process and the principles of the rule of law.
